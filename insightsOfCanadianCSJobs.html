Insights of Canadian CS Jobs

GITHUB REPO:

  • GitHub Repo

This project provides data-driven insights into the Canadian Computer Science job market by analyzing job postings from multiple sources, including LinkedIn and Kaggle datasets. It helps job seekers and recruiters understand hiring trends, demand-supply ratios, seasonal variations, and industry preferences.

Features

1. Opportunity To Applicant Ratio [VS Prior Industry] - Analyzes job postings vs. application volume across sectors gives the insight of interest as well as opportunity across comparing to the prior one selected in the table.
2. Opportunities Across Seasons - Compares job postings by season (Fall, Winter, Spring, Summer) allows to prepare season with most opportunities.
3. Opportunities Across Provinces - Maps job demand across Canadian provinces displays opportunities in provinces.
4. Opportunities Across Industries - Groups job roles by category (Software Development, Data Science, AI, QA, Cloud, etc.) creates opportunity to understand different industries.
5. Interest Across Industries - Groups job roles by category (Software Development, Data Science, AI, QA, Cloud, etc.) along with segregatation of industries.
6. Opportunities Across Experiences - Groups job roles by experience level (Entry, Mid, Senior, Lead, etc.) allowing to know industry's interest.
7. Change in Demand Over Time - Analysis the change in rate of job posting over the time allowing to understand which industry is growing.

Tools & Technologies Used

1. Python (Pandas, NumPy) - Data cleaning and preprocessing.
2. Tableau - Dashboard creation and interactive visualizations.

Key Insights

1. Most opportunities exist in Technology and Software Development sectors.
2. Hiring peaks during Winter, with notable dips in Spring.
3. Ontario and British Columbia are job hotspots in the Canadian CS job market.
4. Entry-level and senior positions dominate the market, with mid-level roles showing steady demand.

How to Access the Dashboard

1. Clone this repository.
2. Open the Tableau workbook (Insights_of_Canadian_CS_Jobs.twbx) using Tableau Public or Tableau Desktop.
3. Explore the interactive visualizations and insights.
4. Review the Keynote presentation (Dashboard_Presentation.key) for a concise overview of findings.
